Slow Cooker Buffalo Chicken Lettuce Wraps

Quick, easy, and healthier than traditional Buffalo wings! Perfect for family dinners, BBQ parties, football Sundays, or just about any event!

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds skinless, boneless chicken breasts
  • 1 (12 ounce) bottle cayenne pepper sauce (such as Frank's® RedHot®)
  • 1 (1 ounce) package ranch dressing mix
  • 1 head Boston lettuce leaves

Directions

  1. Put chicken into the crock of a slow cooker.
  2. Stir cayenne pepper sauce and ranch dressing mix together in a bowl until smooth; pour over chicken.
  3. Cook on Low for 6 to 7 hours.
  4. Strain liquid from slow cooker crock into a bowl to serve on the side.
  5. Spoon chicken into lettuce leaves and roll lettuce around chicken.

Chef's Notes

  • *You can substitute lettuce for tortilla wraps or potato rolls to switch things up. You will be adding calories and crabs but it does give it a nice taste!
  • *Cook on High for 4 hours, if preferred.
